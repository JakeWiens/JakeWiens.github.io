About us

We're a small team located in Tempe Arizona who all have experienced excessive heat all of our lives.


We've all tried typical car shades but they fail to go the extra mile, and that's what inspired us to work on Hot Wave.


We developed Hot Wave to do what car shades out there don't. To protect your entire interior and provide a better experience when getting back into the vehicle after a long day. This is a product all of us have been longing for, and we think you will enjoy it too.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions:


1. How does Hot Wave work?

A: Hot wave covers the interior of a vehicle with a protective material that deflects heat and keeps the sun from damaging the vehicle while also keeping it cool!


2. How much will Hot Wave cost?

A: Hot Wave won't be an expensive product, we aim to have similar costs to typical car shades on the market today.


3.When will Hot Wave release?

A: Hot Wave is a work in progress and is currently in development phases. We're spending the time to make sure we select the right materials and make the best design so our product does all we know that it can. That being said, It's early phases will be done by the end of 2017, and we should have a sell-able product early 2018.


4. Will Hot Wave have different color designs?

A: Eventually we plan to implement different design patterns on the product, but the initial version of the product will not have any different color options.


5. Does Hot Wave work in larger vehicles?

A: Yes, Hot Wave has a design that accommodates all standard vehicle interiors.


6. Where can I buy Hot Wave?

A: Hot Wave will initially be available online through sales. We also intend to have commercial retailers have our product in stock as well, though no deals are currently in place.
